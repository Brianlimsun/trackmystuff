Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a lost item? +
Navigate to the Lost & Found section, click on "Report Lost Item", and fill in the details about your lost item including description, location, and date.
How do QR codes work? +
Generate a unique QR code for your item, attach it, and when someone finds it, they can scan the code to access your contact information.
Is my data secure? +
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect all user data and personal information.
